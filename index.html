Background

Radiation therapy is usually divided or “fractionated” over a treatment course lasting multiple weeks. Fractionation in the context of radiotherapy is the process of dividing a dose of radiation into multiple “fractions”. During this period, the cancerous region or the target area may gradually shrink.Thus we should adjust our radiation therapy plan to protect normal tissue away from radiation exposure and focus on the cancerous area.To realize it, we need to register two images in different period. through image ,registration ,we will have a clear knowledge about how the organ or the target area deforms.
